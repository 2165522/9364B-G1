Anti-rabies Shots

Bakakeng Norte/Sur Barangay is aware about the welfare for animals, one way to show and support the awareness is that they conduct anti-rabbies/vaccination for the animals like dogs and cats to prevent diseases. If a person got bitten bythe said animals, Anti-rabbies of the barangay is also the compliance to Republic Act No. 9482 "Anti Rabbies Act of 2007"

Responsible Pet Ownership

The Barangay always reminds each citizen who owns a pet that they are responsible to bring their pet/s into their home and that they are on leash. Citizens should discipline their pets and must ensure that their pets wouldn't disturb their neighbors.

They also inform citizens that they should not abandon their pets and that animal cruelty is not done.

Stray Dogs' Impounding

Stray dogs wandering aroung the baranggay are captured and taken cared of for the safety of the citizens.
