Tree Planting

Barangay officials, volunteers, and the youth gather for a tree-planting project in the Barangay.

Volunteers work hand-in-hand during the tree-planting activity.

Clean-up Drive

The barangay council makes sure that the roads of Bakakeng are clean, and safe for the motorists by conducting clean-up drives.

To make the surroundings of Bakakeng clean, and for the citizens to breath fresh air, the barangay council conducts clean-up drives to remove trashes along roads.
